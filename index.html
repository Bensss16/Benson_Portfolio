Project 4: YOLO Real-Time Object Detection Models

Developed multiple YOLO-based object detection pipelines for real-world applications. Click below to view demo videos hosted on Google Drive:

  • 🎥 YOLO Demo Video 1 — Real-time detection of people waiting at a counter
  • 🎥 YOLO Demo Video 2 — Body structure detection & tracking
  • 🎥 YOLO Demo Video 3 — License-plate pipeline (Layer 1: car detection)
  • 🎥 YOLO Demo Video 4 — License-plate pipeline (Layer 2: plate detection)
  • 🎥 YOLO Demo Video 5 — End-to-end license plate recognition

    The final model is a three-layer pipeline that combines detection with OCR:

    1. Layer 1 — Car detection (YOLO): Detects cars in the frame and programmatically crops/zooms to each car.
    2. Layer 2 — Plate detection (YOLO): Finds the license plate within the cropped car image and grabs a clean plate patch (screenshot/crop).
    3. Layer 3 — OCR (InternVL3): Sends the plate patch to InternVL3 (OCR) to read and output the exact symbols on the plate.

    This layered approach enables accurate, real-time end-to-end recognition from car → plate → characters.
